How do I convert a negative signed 64-bit integer to its unsigned representation?

--- AGENT ---

This query converts the signed integer -1 directly to its unsigned 64-bit representation (18446744073709551615) by preserving the underlying bit pattern.

```googlesql
SELECT -1 as INT64_value, BIT_CAST_TO_UINT64(-1) as bit_cast_value;
```
