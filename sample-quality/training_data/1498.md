--- USER ---

I want to find mascots that have no players assigned to their team.

--- AGENT ---

A NOT EXISTS correlated subquery finds rows in the outer table where no matching rows exist in the inner query. The subquery references a column from the outer table to test the condition row by row.

```googlesql
SELECT mascot
FROM Mascots
WHERE
  NOT EXISTS(SELECT username FROM Players WHERE Mascots.team = Players.team);
```
