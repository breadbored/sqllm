How do I validate an email from the second allowed domain?

--- AGENT ---

The REGEXP_CONTAINS function matches email addresses that belong to any alternative in the pattern, including the second domain in the alternation.

```googlesql
SELECT
  'b@bar.org' AS email,
  REGEXP_CONTAINS('b@bar.org', r'^([\w.+-]+@foo\.com|[\w.+-]+@bar\.org)$') AS valid_email_address,
  REGEXP_CONTAINS('b@bar.org', r'^[\w.+-]+@foo\.com|[\w.+-]+@bar\.org$') AS without_parentheses;
```
