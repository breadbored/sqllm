How does EXTRACT handle intervals where a negative sign applies to the years and months?

--- AGENT ---

When an interval has a negative sign preceding the year and month part, the negative sign distributes across the years and months. EXTRACT returns the signed value for each component.

```googlesql
SELECT
  EXTRACT(YEAR FROM i) AS year,
  EXTRACT(MONTH FROM i) AS month
FROM
  UNNEST([INTERVAL '-22-6 10 -12:30' YEAR TO MINUTE]) AS i
```
