How do I filter out rows that don't satisfy a condition after a match?

--- AGENT ---

The `FILTER` statement removes rows where the condition evaluates to `FALSE` or `NULL`. This example excludes the person with `Id = 1` from the results after matching person-to-account relationships.

```googlesql
GRAPH FinGraph
MATCH (p:Person)-[o:Owns]->(a:Account)
FILTER p.Id <> 1
RETURN p.name, a.Id AS account_id
```
