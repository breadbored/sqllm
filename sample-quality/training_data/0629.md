How do I aggregate over group variables and then collect the results per destination account?

--- AGENT ---

You can compute horizontal aggregates from group variables in a `LET` clause, then use vertical aggregation with `ARRAY_AGG` to collect those values grouped by other columns. The horizontal aggregate is evaluated per path, and the vertical aggregate combines results by the grouping columns.

```googlesql
GRAPH FinGraph
MATCH (src:Account {id: 7})-[e:Transfers]->{1,2}(dst:Account)
LET total_amount = SUM(e.amount)
RETURN
  src.id AS source_account_id, dst.id AS destination_account_id,
  ARRAY_AGG(total_amount) as total_amounts_per_path
```
