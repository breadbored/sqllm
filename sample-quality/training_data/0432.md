How do I calculate the difference between two dates using different week definitions?

--- AGENT ---

`DATE_DIFF()` supports WEEK (Sunday-based), WEEK(MONDAY) (Monday-based), and ISOWEEK (ISO 8601) granularities, each counting different week boundaries. The choice of week definition affects which boundaries are counted between two dates.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATE_DIFF('2017-12-18', '2017-12-17', WEEK) AS week_diff,
  DATE_DIFF('2017-12-18', '2017-12-17', WEEK(MONDAY)) AS week_weekday_diff,
  DATE_DIFF('2017-12-18', '2017-12-17', ISOWEEK) AS isoweek_diff;
```
