How do I combine two queries with different column orders by matching names instead of positions?

--- AGENT ---

`UNION ALL BY NAME` matches columns by name across the two queries instead of by position, allowing columns to be in different orders.

```googlesql
SELECT 1 AS one_digit, 10 AS two_digit
UNION ALL BY NAME
SELECT 20 AS two_digit, 2 AS one_digit;
```
