How do I get the last day of a week when the week starts on Sunday?

--- AGENT ---

LAST_DAY with WEEK(SUNDAY) returns the last day of the week containing the given datetime, where weeks start on Sunday.

```googlesql
SELECT LAST_DAY(DATETIME '2008-11-10 15:30:00', WEEK(SUNDAY)) AS last_day
```
