How do I define a date range literal with a lower and upper bound?

--- AGENT ---

A RANGE literal with a date type contains the RANGE keyword, type parameter, and a string with lower and upper bounds in square brackets separated by a single space and comma. The bounds use square bracket for inclusive and parenthesis for exclusive bounds.

```googlesql
RANGE<DATE> '[2020-01-01, 2020-12-31)'
```
