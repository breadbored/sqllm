How do I check which ISO year a late-December timestamp belongs to?

--- AGENT ---

The `EXTRACT` function with `ISOYEAR` returns the ISO 8601 week-numbering year, which can differ from the Gregorian calendar year, especially around year boundaries. For dates in late December, the ISO year may extend into what would typically be considered the next Gregorian year.

```googlesql
SELECT
  TIMESTAMP("2007-12-31 12:00:00+00") AS timestamp_value,
  EXTRACT(ISOYEAR FROM TIMESTAMP("2007-12-31 12:00:00+00")) AS isoyear,
  EXTRACT(ISOWEEK FROM TIMESTAMP("2007-12-31 12:00:00+00")) AS isoweek,
  EXTRACT(YEAR FROM TIMESTAMP("2007-12-31 12:00:00+00")) AS year,
  EXTRACT(WEEK FROM TIMESTAMP("2007-12-31 12:00:00+00")) AS week
```
