How do I convert a negative 64-bit integer to its unsigned representation and back to signed, preserving the bit pattern?

--- AGENT ---

This query demonstrates 64-bit bit casting by converting -1 to its unsigned representation (18446744073709551615) and back to INT64, showing that the underlying bit pattern is preserved regardless of how the value is interpreted as signed or unsigned.

```googlesql
SELECT BIT_CAST_TO_UINT64(-1) as UINT64_value, BIT_CAST_TO_INT64(BIT_CAST_TO_UINT64(-1)) as bit_cast_value;
```
