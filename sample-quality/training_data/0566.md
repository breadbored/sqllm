How do I test whether a polygon contains a point, distinguishing between points on the boundary versus the interior?

--- AGENT ---

`ST_CONTAINS` returns TRUE if a point lies strictly within a polygon's interior (not on the boundary), and FALSE for points outside or on the boundary.

```googlesql
SELECT
  ST_GEOGPOINT(i, i) AS p,
  ST_CONTAINS(ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('POLYGON((1 1, 20 1, 10 20, 1 1))'),
              ST_GEOGPOINT(i, i)) AS `contains`
FROM UNNEST([0, 1, 10]) AS i;
```
