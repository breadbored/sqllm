How can I normalize an interval's day component to a standard range, adjusting the month or year accordingly?

--- AGENT ---

JUSTIFY_DAYS normalizes the day part of an interval to the range -29 to 29 by automatically adjusting the month and year components as needed. This function is useful for ensuring consistent interval representation where days don't exceed the standard range.

```googlesql
SELECT
  JUSTIFY_DAYS(INTERVAL 29 DAY) AS i1,
  JUSTIFY_DAYS(INTERVAL -30 DAY) AS i2,
  JUSTIFY_DAYS(INTERVAL 31 DAY) AS i3,
  JUSTIFY_DAYS(INTERVAL -65 DAY) AS i4,
  JUSTIFY_DAYS(INTERVAL 370 DAY) AS i5
```
