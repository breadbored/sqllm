How do I verify what ISO week and ISO year a specific January 2nd timestamp belongs to?

--- AGENT ---

The `EXTRACT` function returns both the ISO year and ISO week number for any timestamp, allowing you to confirm which ISO period a date falls within. January 2nd is typically in ISO week 1 of the calendar year.

```googlesql
SELECT
  TIMESTAMP("2017-01-02 12:00:00+00") AS timestamp_value,
  EXTRACT(ISOYEAR FROM TIMESTAMP("2017-01-02 12:00:00+00")) AS isoyear,
  EXTRACT(ISOWEEK FROM TIMESTAMP("2017-01-02 12:00:00+00")) AS isoweek,
  EXTRACT(YEAR FROM TIMESTAMP("2017-01-02 12:00:00+00")) AS year,
  EXTRACT(WEEK FROM TIMESTAMP("2017-01-02 12:00:00+00")) AS week
```
