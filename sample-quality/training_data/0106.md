How do I find the top 2 items by their total weighted sum?

--- AGENT ---

APPROX_TOP_SUM returns the top N items ranked by the sum of their weight values, with each result as a struct containing the `value` and its approximate total `sum`. This is useful for finding dominant categories weighted by an associated metric like revenue or duration.

```googlesql
SELECT APPROX_TOP_SUM(x, weight, 2) AS approx_top_sum FROM
UNNEST([
  STRUCT("apple" AS x, 3 AS weight),
  ("pear", 2),
  ("apple", 0),
  ("banana", 5),
  ("pear", 4)
]);
```
