Can I retrieve the current date using a UTC offset instead of a time zone name?

--- AGENT ---

`CURRENT_DATE()` also accepts UTC offset strings like `'-08'` as the time zone argument, allowing you to specify the date relative to a fixed offset from UTC.

```googlesql
SELECT CURRENT_DATE('-08') AS the_date;
```
