What happens to timestamps during daylight saving time when an hour is skipped?

--- AGENT ---

When DST transitions forward and an hour is skipped (e.g., 2 AM to 3 AM), civil times in the skipped hour are treated as if one hour later. The two different civil times resolve to the same absolute timestamp.

```googlesql
SELECT
FORMAT_TIMESTAMP("%c %Z", "2024-03-10 02:30:00 America/Los_Angeles", "UTC") AS two_thirty,
FORMAT_TIMESTAMP("%c %Z", "2024-03-10 03:30:00 America/Los_Angeles", "UTC") AS three_thirty;
```
