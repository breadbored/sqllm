How can I normalize both the day and time components of an interval in a single operation?

--- AGENT ---

JUSTIFY_INTERVAL normalizes both the day and time parts of an interval simultaneously, adjusting year and month components as needed to bring all components into their standard ranges.

```googlesql
SELECT JUSTIFY_INTERVAL(INTERVAL '29 49:00:00' DAY TO SECOND) AS i
```
