How do I compare the difference in days versus weeks for two DATETIME values?

--- AGENT ---

DATETIME_DIFF with different granularities counts the number of boundaries for each unit. This example shows that a 24-hour difference results in 1 day but also 1 week, because there is a week boundary (Sunday) between Saturday and Sunday.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATETIME_DIFF(DATETIME '2017-10-15 00:00:00',
    DATETIME '2017-10-14 00:00:00', DAY) as days_diff,
  DATETIME_DIFF(DATETIME '2017-10-15 00:00:00',
    DATETIME '2017-10-14 00:00:00', WEEK) as weeks_diff;
```
