How can I test whether division will succeed without causing an error?

--- AGENT ---

ISERROR evaluates the division expression and returns FALSE if it succeeds without error. Division of 2 by 1 produces no error.

```googlesql
SELECT ISERROR(2/1) AS is_error
```
