How do I access a nested field within a top-level extension?

--- AGENT ---

This query accesses a top-level extension and then uses the dot operator to extract a specific field from the extension's nested message. The expression `foo_field.(some.package.point).y` retrieves the `y` field from the `point` extension.

```googlesql
SELECT
  foo_field.(some.package.point).y
FROM
  Test;
```
