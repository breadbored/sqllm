How do I compare date differences across calendar years using both Gregorian and ISO years?

--- AGENT ---

`DATE_DIFF()` with YEAR granularity counts Gregorian calendar year boundaries, while ISOYEAR counts ISO 8601 week-numbering year boundaries, which can differ by up to one year depending on when dates fall relative to ISO week boundaries.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATE_DIFF('2017-12-30', '2014-12-30', YEAR) AS year_diff,
  DATE_DIFF('2017-12-30', '2014-12-30', ISOYEAR) AS isoyear_diff;
```
