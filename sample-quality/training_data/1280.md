How do I split a date range into 2-day subranges, including any partial range at the end?

--- AGENT ---

GENERATE_RANGE_ARRAY splits a range into subranges of the specified interval size. When the remaining portion is smaller than the step interval, it is included as a partial subrange by default. This query splits a 5-day range into 2-day subranges with a 1-day partial range at the end.

```googlesql
SELECT GENERATE_RANGE_ARRAY(
  RANGE(DATE '2020-01-01', DATE '2020-01-06'),
  INTERVAL 2 DAY) AS results;
```
