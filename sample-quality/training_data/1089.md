How do I add ordering to an aggregation as a separate step?

--- AGENT ---

After the `AGGREGATE` operator, you can add a separate `ORDER BY` pipe operator to sort the results. This is equivalent to using `GROUP AND ORDER BY` but makes the ordering step explicit.

```googlesql
FROM Produce
|> AGGREGATE SUM(sales) AS total_sales
   GROUP BY category, item
|> ORDER BY category, item DESC;
```
