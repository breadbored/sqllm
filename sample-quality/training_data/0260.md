How do I cast a string with UNBOUNDED as one boundary to a RANGE<DATE>?

--- AGENT ---

CAST to RANGE<DATE> supports the keyword UNBOUNDED to represent an infinite boundary, allowing you to specify a range with one unbounded side and one concrete date boundary.

```googlesql
SELECT CAST(
  '[UNBOUNDED, 2020-01-02)'
  AS RANGE<DATE>) AS string_to_range
```
