How do I get π with higher precision as a BIGNUMERIC type?

--- AGENT ---

PI_BIGNUMERIC() returns the mathematical constant π as a BIGNUMERIC value, providing much higher precision than the DOUBLE variant. This query retrieves π with arbitrary-precision decimal arithmetic:

```googlesql
SELECT PI_BIGNUMERIC() AS pi
```
