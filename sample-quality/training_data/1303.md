How do I extract the lower bound (start date) from a date range?

--- AGENT ---

RANGE_START extracts the lower boundary value from a range. This query retrieves the start date from a date range, returning the lower bound value.

```googlesql
SELECT RANGE_START(RANGE<DATE> '[2022-12-01, 2022-12-31)') AS results;
```
