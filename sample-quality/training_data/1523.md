How do I extract the day of a month from a timestamp, and how does the result differ when using different time zones?

--- AGENT ---

The `EXTRACT` function with the `DAY` part returns the day component from a timestamp, and you can use `AT TIME ZONE` to control which time zone interprets the timestamp before extraction. The same absolute timestamp may correspond to different days depending on the time zone.

```googlesql
SELECT
  EXTRACT(
    DAY
    FROM TIMESTAMP('2008-12-25 05:30:00+00') AT TIME ZONE 'UTC')
    AS the_day_utc,
  EXTRACT(
    DAY
    FROM TIMESTAMP('2008-12-25 05:30:00+00') AT TIME ZONE 'America/Los_Angeles')
    AS the_day_california
```
