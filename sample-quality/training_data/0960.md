How do I create a datetime range with both lower and upper bounds?

--- AGENT ---

A RANGE literal with datetime type specifies the complete date and time for both the lower and upper bounds, allowing you to define a time interval with specific start and end points.

```googlesql
RANGE<DATETIME> '[2020-01-01 12:00:00, 2020-12-31 12:00:00)'
```
