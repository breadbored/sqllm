How do I cast an INTERVAL with a year-to-second range to a STRING?

--- AGENT ---

CAST from INTERVAL to STRING preserves the full precision of the interval components when converting to text format, producing output that includes years, months, days, and all time components.

```googlesql
SELECT CAST(
  INTERVAL "1-2 3 4:5:6.789" YEAR TO SECOND
  AS STRING) AS interval_to_string
```
