How do I format a DATETIME value as abbreviated month, day, and year?

--- AGENT ---

FORMAT_DATETIME applies a format string to a DATETIME value. The format string %b-%d-%Y produces the abbreviated month name, day, and four-digit year separated by hyphens.

```googlesql
SELECT
  FORMAT_DATETIME("%b-%d-%Y", DATETIME "2008-12-25 15:30:00")
  AS formatted;
```
