How do I extract the outer ring of a polygon as a linestring?

--- AGENT ---

`ST_EXTERIORRING` returns the outermost boundary of a polygon geography as a linestring geography, excluding any interior holes.

```googlesql
WITH geo as
 (SELECT ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('POLYGON((0 0, 1 4, 2 2, 0 0))') AS g UNION ALL
  SELECT ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('''POLYGON((1 1, 1 10, 5 10, 5 1, 1 1),
                                  (2 2, 3 4, 2 4, 2 2))''') as g)
SELECT ST_EXTERIORRING(g) AS ring FROM geo;
```
