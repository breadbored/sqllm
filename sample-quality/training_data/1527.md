How do I check the ISO year for a timestamp on the last day of a year?

--- AGENT ---

A timestamp on December 31st may belong to the next ISO year if it falls after the Thursday that begins the last ISO week of the Gregorian year. The `EXTRACT` function with `ISOYEAR` and `ISOWEEK` reveals this boundary crossing.

```googlesql
SELECT
  TIMESTAMP("2009-12-31 12:00:00+00") AS timestamp_value,
  EXTRACT(ISOYEAR FROM TIMESTAMP("2009-12-31 12:00:00+00")) AS isoyear,
  EXTRACT(ISOWEEK FROM TIMESTAMP("2009-12-31 12:00:00+00")) AS isoweek,
  EXTRACT(YEAR FROM TIMESTAMP("2009-12-31 12:00:00+00")) AS year,
  EXTRACT(WEEK FROM TIMESTAMP("2009-12-31 12:00:00+00")) AS week
```
