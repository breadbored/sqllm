How do I round a date down to the first day of its month?

--- AGENT ---

`DATE_TRUNC()` truncates a DATE value to a specified granularity, rounding down to the beginning of that period. Using MONTH granularity returns the first day of the month.

```googlesql
SELECT DATE_TRUNC(DATE '2008-12-25', MONTH) AS month;
```
