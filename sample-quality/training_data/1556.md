How do I subtract a time interval from a timestamp?

--- AGENT ---

The `TIMESTAMP_SUB` function takes a timestamp and subtracts a specified number of time units (like minutes, hours, or days) from it. This operation is independent of any time zone.

```googlesql
SELECT
  TIMESTAMP("2008-12-25 15:30:00+00") AS original,
  TIMESTAMP_SUB(TIMESTAMP "2008-12-25 15:30:00+00", INTERVAL 10 MINUTE) AS earlier;
```
