How do I truncate a date to the beginning of the week, with the week starting on Monday?

--- AGENT ---

`DATE_TRUNC()` with WEEK(MONDAY) granularity rounds a date down to the preceding Monday, allowing you to find the start of a custom-defined week.

```googlesql
SELECT date AS original, DATE_TRUNC(date, WEEK(MONDAY)) AS truncated
FROM (SELECT DATE('2017-11-05') AS date);
```
