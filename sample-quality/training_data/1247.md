How do I join two tables and return rows that exist in both?

--- AGENT ---

`INNER JOIN` (or simply `JOIN`) combines rows from two tables based on a join condition, returning only rows where the condition is true.

```googlesql
SELECT Roster.LastName, TeamMascot.Mascot
FROM Roster JOIN TeamMascot ON Roster.SchoolID = TeamMascot.SchoolID;
```
