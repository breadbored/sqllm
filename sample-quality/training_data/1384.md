How do I find overlapping occurrences of a substring?

--- AGENT ---

The INSTR function with the occurrence parameter includes overlapping matches; for example, searching for 'oo' in 'helloooo' finds the first at position 5 and the second at position 6.

```googlesql
SELECT
  'helloooo' AS value, 'oo' AS subvalue, 1 AS position, 1 AS occurrence,
  INSTR('helloooo', 'oo', 1, 1) AS instr;
```
