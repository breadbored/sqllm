How do I compute the difference between two dates as an interval?

--- AGENT ---

The datetime subtraction operator subtracts two date, timestamp, or datetime values to produce an INTERVAL representing the time difference between them.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATE "2021-05-20" - DATE "2020-04-19" AS date_diff,
  TIMESTAMP "2021-06-01 12:34:56.789" - TIMESTAMP "2021-05-31 00:00:00" AS time_diff
```
