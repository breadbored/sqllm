How do I extract the second half of a linestring as a new linestring segment?

--- AGENT ---

`ST_LINESUBSTRING` extracts a portion of a linestring between two fractions (0 to 1) of its total length, returning a linestring or point depending on whether multiple points result.

```googlesql
WITH data AS (
  SELECT ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('LINESTRING(20 70, 70 60, 10 70, 70 70)') AS geo1
)
SELECT ST_LINESUBSTRING(geo1, 0.5, 1) AS segment
FROM data;
```
