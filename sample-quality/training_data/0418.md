How do I convert a UUID value to a string?

--- AGENT ---

Use CAST(uuid_value AS STRING) to convert a UUID to its 36-character lowercase ASCII representation in the format 8-4-4-4-12 digits separated by hyphens.

```googlesql
SELECT CAST(NEW_UUID() AS STRING) AS UUID_STR;
```
