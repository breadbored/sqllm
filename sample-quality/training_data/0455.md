How do I convert a DATE value to its Unix epoch representation (number of days since 1970)?

--- AGENT ---

`UNIX_DATE()` returns the number of days between a DATE value and January 1, 1970, allowing you to convert dates to their Unix epoch day number.

```googlesql
SELECT UNIX_DATE(DATE '2008-12-25') AS days_from_epoch;
```
