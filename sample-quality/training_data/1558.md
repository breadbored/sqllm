How do I truncate a timestamp to a custom week start day in a non-UTC time zone?

--- AGENT ---

The `TIMESTAMP_TRUNC` function supports `WEEK(WEEKDAY)` syntax to truncate to the first day of the week starting on a specific weekday. You can combine this with a time zone to perform the truncation in that zone's civil time.

```googlesql
SELECT
  timestamp_value AS timestamp_value,
  TIMESTAMP_TRUNC(timestamp_value, WEEK(MONDAY), "UTC") AS utc_truncated,
  TIMESTAMP_TRUNC(timestamp_value, WEEK(MONDAY), "Pacific/Auckland") AS nzdt_truncated
FROM (SELECT TIMESTAMP("2017-11-06 00:00:00+12") AS timestamp_value);
```
