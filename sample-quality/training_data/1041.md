How do I add an interval to a date or timestamp?

--- AGENT ---

The interval arithmetic operator adds an interval to a date, timestamp, or datetime value, or subtracts an interval from these types to shift them by a specified duration.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATE "2021-04-20" + INTERVAL 25 HOUR AS date_plus,
  TIMESTAMP "2021-05-02 00:01:02.345+00" - INTERVAL 10 SECOND AS time_minus
```
