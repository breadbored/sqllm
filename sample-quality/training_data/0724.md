How do I match transfers originating from an account owned by a specific person?

--- AGENT ---

Multi-hop patterns in `MATCH` can traverse multiple edge types to filter results based on indirect relationships. This example matches transfers from accounts owned by the person with `id = 2`.

```googlesql
GRAPH FinGraph
MATCH
  (p:Person {id: 2})-[:Owns]->(account:Account)-[t:Transfers]->
  (to_account:Account)
RETURN p.id AS sender_id, to_account.id AS to_id
```
