How can I normalize an interval's time components to a standard range, adjusting the day component if needed?

--- AGENT ---

JUSTIFY_HOURS normalizes the time part of an interval to the range -23:59:59.999999 to 23:59:59.999999 by automatically adjusting the day component. This ensures that hours, minutes, and seconds stay within their standard ranges.

```googlesql
SELECT
  JUSTIFY_HOURS(INTERVAL 23 HOUR) AS i1,
  JUSTIFY_HOURS(INTERVAL -24 HOUR) AS i2,
  JUSTIFY_HOURS(INTERVAL 47 HOUR) AS i3,
  JUSTIFY_HOURS(INTERVAL -12345 MINUTE) AS i4
```
