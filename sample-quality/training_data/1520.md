How can I subtract a time interval from an existing TIME value?

--- AGENT ---

TIME_SUB() subtracts a specified interval from a TIME value and automatically adjusts for the 24-hour boundary. This example subtracts 10 minutes from 15:30:00, resulting in 15:20:00.

```googlesql
SELECT
  TIME "15:30:00" as original_date,
  TIME_SUB(TIME "15:30:00", INTERVAL 10 MINUTE) as earlier;
```
