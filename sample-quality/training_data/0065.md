How do I compute the bitwise AND across multiple integer values?

--- AGENT ---

`BIT_AND` performs a bitwise AND operation on all values in a group. This example performs bitwise AND on two hexadecimal numbers:

```googlesql
SELECT BIT_AND(x) as bit_and FROM UNNEST([0xF001, 0x00A1]) as x;
```
