How do I find the intersection of a date range with an unbounded upper limit and a bounded date range?

--- AGENT ---

RANGE_INTERSECT finds the overlapping segment of two ranges, including cases where one range has no upper boundary. This query intersects [2022-02-01, UNBOUNDED) with [2021-06-15, 2022-04-15), resulting in the bounded intersection [2022-02-01, 2022-04-15).

```googlesql
SELECT RANGE_INTERSECT(
  RANGE<DATE> '[2022-02-01, UNBOUNDED)',
  RANGE<DATE> '[2021-06-15, 2022-04-15)') AS results;
```
