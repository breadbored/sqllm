How do I check whether a polygon covers a point, including points on the boundary?

--- AGENT ---

`ST_COVERS` returns TRUE if all points of a geography lie on the boundary or interior of another geography, making it more inclusive than `ST_CONTAINS` by allowing boundary points.

```googlesql
SELECT
  ST_GEOGPOINT(i, i) AS p,
  ST_COVERS(ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('POLYGON((1 1, 20 1, 10 20, 1 1))'),
            ST_GEOGPOINT(i, i)) AS `covers`
FROM UNNEST([0, 1, 10]) AS i;
```
