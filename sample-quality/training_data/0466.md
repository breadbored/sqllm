How do I truncate a DATETIME to the beginning of an ISO 8601 year?

--- AGENT ---

DATETIME_TRUNC with ISOYEAR rounds a DATETIME to the first day of the ISO week-numbering year, which may fall in the previous Gregorian calendar year. This example shows how a date in June 2015 truncates to an ISO year that began in December 2014.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATETIME_TRUNC('2015-06-15 00:00:00', ISOYEAR) AS isoyear_boundary,
  EXTRACT(ISOYEAR FROM DATETIME '2015-06-15 00:00:00') AS isoyear_number;
```
