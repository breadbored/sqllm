How do I construct a DATETIME value from individual year, month, day, hour, minute, and second components?

--- AGENT ---

The DATETIME function can construct a DATETIME object from individual numeric values for year, month, day, hour, minute, and second, or from a timestamp with a specified time zone conversion.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATETIME(2008, 12, 25, 05, 30, 00) as datetime_ymdhms,
  DATETIME(TIMESTAMP "2008-12-25 05:30:00+00", "America/Los_Angeles") as datetime_tstz;
```
