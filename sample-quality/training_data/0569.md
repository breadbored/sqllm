How do I compute the part of one polygon that does not overlap with another polygon?

--- AGENT ---

`ST_DIFFERENCE` returns the point set difference between two geographies, producing a geography consisting of the parts of the first geography that do not intersect with the second.

```googlesql
SELECT
  ST_DIFFERENCE(
      ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('POLYGON((0 0, 10 0, 10 10, 0 0))'),
      ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('POLYGON((4 2, 6 2, 8 6, 4 2))')
  );
```
