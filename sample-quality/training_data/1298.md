How do I verify that two date ranges do not overlap?

--- AGENT ---

RANGE_OVERLAPS returns FALSE if the ranges do not intersect. This query shows that [2020-02-01, 2020-09-01) and [2021-06-15, 2022-04-15) do not overlap because the first range ends before the second range begins.

```googlesql
SELECT RANGE_OVERLAPS(
  RANGE<DATE> '[2020-02-01, 2020-09-01)',
  RANGE<DATE> '[2021-06-15, 2022-04-15)') AS results;
```
