What happens when the inner IFERROR's catch expression also produces an error?

--- AGENT ---

When the inner IFERROR evaluates ERROR('b') in its catch expression and that produces an error, the outer IFERROR catches that error and returns its catch value ('c').

```googlesql
SELECT IFERROR(IFERROR(ERROR('a'), ERROR('b')), 'c') AS result
```
