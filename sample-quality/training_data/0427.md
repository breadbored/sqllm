How do I construct a DATE value from year, month, and day components?

--- AGENT ---

The `DATE()` function constructs a DATE value from separate year, month, and day integers, or extracts a date from a timestamp or datetime expression with optional time zone conversion.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATE(2016, 12, 25) AS date_ymd,
  DATE(DATETIME '2016-12-25 23:59:59') AS date_dt,
  DATE(TIMESTAMP '2016-12-25 05:30:00+07', 'America/Los_Angeles') AS date_tstz;
```
