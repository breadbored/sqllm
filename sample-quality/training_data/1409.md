How do I validate an email that does not match either allowed domain?

--- AGENT ---

When an email's domain doesn't match any of the alternatives in the full-match pattern, REGEXP_CONTAINS returns FALSE.

```googlesql
SELECT
  'a@foo.computer' AS email,
  REGEXP_CONTAINS('a@foo.computer', r'^([\w.+-]+@foo\.com|[\w.+-]+@bar\.org)$') AS valid_email_address,
  REGEXP_CONTAINS('a@foo.computer', r'^[\w.+-]+@foo\.com|[\w.+-]+@bar\.org$') AS without_parentheses;
```
