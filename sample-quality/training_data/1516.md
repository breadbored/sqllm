How can I construct a TIME value from integer hour/minute/second components or convert a timestamp with timezone conversion?

--- AGENT ---

The TIME() constructor accepts either hour/minute/second integers, or a TIMESTAMP with optional timezone conversion. This example shows both forms: constructing from integers (15, 30, 00) and converting a timestamp in +08 timezone to America/Los_Angeles timezone, which shifts the time to 23:30:00.

```googlesql
SELECT
  TIME(15, 30, 00) as time_hms,
  TIME(TIMESTAMP "2008-12-25 15:30:00+08", "America/Los_Angeles") as time_tstz;
```
