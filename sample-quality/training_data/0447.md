How do I find the last day of a week that starts on Sunday?

--- AGENT ---

`LAST_DAY()` with WEEK(SUNDAY) returns the final day (Saturday) of the week containing the input date, using Sunday as the week start.

```googlesql
SELECT LAST_DAY(DATE '2008-11-10', WEEK(SUNDAY)) AS last_day
```
