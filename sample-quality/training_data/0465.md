How do I truncate a DATETIME to the beginning of a week starting on Monday?

--- AGENT ---

DATETIME_TRUNC with WEEK(MONDAY) rounds a DATETIME to the beginning of the week that starts on Monday. This example shows a datetime on a Sunday being truncated to the preceding Monday.

```googlesql
SELECT
 datetime AS original,
 DATETIME_TRUNC(datetime, WEEK(MONDAY)) AS truncated
FROM (SELECT DATETIME(TIMESTAMP "2017-11-05 00:00:00+00", "UTC") AS datetime);
```
