How do I designate a specific column as the privacy unit when creating a view for use with differential privacy?

--- AGENT ---

Add the `anonymization_userid_column` option to the view's OPTIONS clause to specify which column represents the privacy unit—the unique identifier for the entity being protected by differential privacy. This identifies the privacy unit column that BigQuery will use during differential privacy operations.

```googlesql
CREATE OR REPLACE VIEW view_on_students
OPTIONS(anonymization_userid_column='id')
AS (SELECT * FROM students);
```
