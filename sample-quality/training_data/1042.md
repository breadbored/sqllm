How do I multiply or divide an interval by an integer?

--- AGENT ---

The interval arithmetic operator supports multiplication and division to scale intervals. You can multiply an interval by an integer to extend it, or divide it to reduce it proportionally.

```googlesql
SELECT
  INTERVAL '1:2:3' HOUR TO SECOND * 10 AS mul1,
  INTERVAL 35 SECOND * 4 AS mul2,
  INTERVAL 10 YEAR / 3 AS div1,
  INTERVAL 1 MONTH / 12 AS div2
```
