How do I construct an interval with a year-to-month range?

--- AGENT ---

An INTERVAL with datetime part range uses a string in the format `'years-months'` with starting and ending datetime part keywords. This allows you to express intervals spanning years and months as a single unit.

```googlesql
INTERVAL '1-2' YEAR TO MONTH
```
