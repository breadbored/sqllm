How do I get the last day of the month without specifying the date part explicitly?

--- AGENT ---

`LAST_DAY()` uses MONTH as the default date part when none is specified, making it simple to find the last day of the month with a single argument.

```googlesql
SELECT LAST_DAY(DATE '2008-11-25') AS last_day
```
