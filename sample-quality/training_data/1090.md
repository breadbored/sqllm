How do I add ordering suffixes to columns in both the AGGREGATE and GROUP BY lists?

--- AGENT ---

Ordering suffixes like `ASC` or `DESC` can be added to columns in both the `AGGREGATE` and `GROUP BY` lists. Suffixes on `GROUP BY` columns are applied first, then suffixes on aggregate columns. This is equivalent to an `ORDER BY` clause combining both sets of columns.

```googlesql
FROM Produce
|> AGGREGATE SUM(sales) AS total_sales ASC
   GROUP BY item, category DESC;
```
