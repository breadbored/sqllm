How do I convert a point geography to a GeoHash string with a specific precision?

--- AGENT ---

`ST_GEOHASH` converts a point geography to a GeoHash string representation with a specified maximum character length controlling the precision of the encoded bounding box.

```googlesql
SELECT ST_GEOHASH(ST_GEOGPOINT(-122.35, 47.62), 10) geohash
```
