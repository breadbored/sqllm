How do I limit the number of values concatenated?

--- AGENT ---

`STRING_AGG` with a `LIMIT` clause concatenates only the first N values. This example joins at most 2 fruit names:

```googlesql
SELECT STRING_AGG(fruit, " & " LIMIT 2) AS string_agg
FROM UNNEST(["apple", "pear", "banana", "pear"]) AS fruit;
```
