How do I find the intersection of two date ranges that both extend indefinitely upward?

--- AGENT ---

RANGE_INTERSECT finds the overlapping segment when both ranges have unbounded upper boundaries. This query intersects [2022-02-01, UNBOUNDED) with [2021-06-15, UNBOUNDED), resulting in [2022-02-01, UNBOUNDED) as the intersection.

```googlesql
SELECT RANGE_INTERSECT(
  RANGE<DATE> '[2022-02-01, UNBOUNDED)',
  RANGE<DATE> '[2021-06-15, UNBOUNDED)') AS results;
```
