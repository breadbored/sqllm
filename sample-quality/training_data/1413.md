--- USER ---

How do I extract the username part of an email address?

--- AGENT ---

The REGEXP_EXTRACT function returns the substring matching a regular expression pattern; without a capturing group, it returns the entire match.

```googlesql
SELECT REGEXP_EXTRACT('foo@example.com', r'^[a-zA-Z0-9_.+-]+') AS user_name
```
