How do I find the overlapping segment of two date ranges?

--- AGENT ---

RANGE_INTERSECT returns the portion of two ranges where they overlap. This query finds the intersection of [2022-02-01, 2022-09-01) and [2021-06-15, 2022-04-15), which is [2022-02-01, 2022-04-15).

```googlesql
SELECT RANGE_INTERSECT(
  RANGE<DATE> '[2022-02-01, 2022-09-01)',
  RANGE<DATE> '[2021-06-15, 2022-04-15)') AS results;
```
