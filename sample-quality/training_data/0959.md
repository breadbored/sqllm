--- USER ---

How do I create a date range that has no lower bound?

--- AGENT ---

A RANGE can have an unbounded or NULL lower bound by using `UNBOUNDED` or `NULL` in the lower bound position. This allows you to represent a range that extends to negative infinity.

```googlesql
RANGE<DATE> '[UNBOUNDED, 2020-12-31)'
```
