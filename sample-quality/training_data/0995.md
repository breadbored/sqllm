How do I compute hyperbolic secant values for different inputs?

--- AGENT ---

SECH computes the hyperbolic secant of an angle in radians and does not produce errors. This query evaluates the hyperbolic secant for three different angles:

```googlesql
SELECT SECH(0.5) AS a, SECH(-2) AS b, SECH(100) AS c;
```
