How can I compare the difference between two dates in both days and weeks?

--- AGENT ---

`DATE_DIFF()` can be called multiple times with different granularities to show the interval between two dates at different time scales. When comparing successive days, WEEK granularity counts the number of Sunday boundaries crossed.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATE_DIFF(DATE '2017-10-15', DATE '2017-10-14', DAY) AS days_diff,
  DATE_DIFF(DATE '2017-10-15', DATE '2017-10-14', WEEK) AS weeks_diff;
```
