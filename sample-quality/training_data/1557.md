How do I truncate a timestamp to a specific day in different time zones?

--- AGENT ---

The `TIMESTAMP_TRUNC` function rounds a timestamp down to a specified granularity and supports an optional time zone parameter. The same timestamp may truncate to different days depending on which time zone's civil time is used for the truncation.

```googlesql
SELECT
  TIMESTAMP_TRUNC(TIMESTAMP "2008-12-25 15:30:00+00", DAY, "UTC") AS utc,
  TIMESTAMP_TRUNC(TIMESTAMP "2008-12-25 15:30:00+00", DAY, "America/Los_Angeles") AS la;
```
