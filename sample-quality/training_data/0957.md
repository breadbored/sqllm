How do I construct a timestamp literal with the ISO 8601 format using T as a separator and Z for UTC?

--- AGENT ---

A timestamp can use `T` or `t` to separate the date and time, and `Z` or `z` to indicate UTC timezone. These optional characters allow you to write timestamps in standard ISO 8601 format.

```googlesql
TIMESTAMP '2017-01-18T12:34:56.123456Z'
```
