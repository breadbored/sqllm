When does GoogleSQL convert a GEOMETRYCOLLECTION to a simpler geometry type?

--- AGENT ---

If a GEOMETRYCOLLECTION can be represented as a simpler geometry type, GoogleSQL automatically simplifies it. A collection of points becomes MULTIPOINT, and other simplifications occur when the structure allows.

```googlesql
SELECT
  ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('MULTIPOINT(1 1, 2 2)') AS a,
  ST_GEOGFROMTEXT('GEOMETRYCOLLECTION(POINT(1 1), POINT(2 2))') AS b
```
