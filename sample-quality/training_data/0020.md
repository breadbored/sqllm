How do I compute a differentially private variance over a view?

--- AGENT ---

This query applies `VAR_POP` with `DIFFERENTIAL_PRIVACY` to a view, computing the population variance with per-row contribution bounds. Noise is added based on the epsilon parameter.

```googlesql
SELECT
  WITH DIFFERENTIAL_PRIVACY
    OPTIONS(epsilon=10, delta=.01, max_groups_contributed=1)
    item,
    VAR_POP(quantity, contribution_bounds_per_row=>(0, 100)) pop_variance
FROM {{USERNAME}}.view_on_professors
GROUP BY item;
```
