How do I create a datetime value representing a specific date and time?

--- AGENT ---

The DATETIME keyword constructs a datetime literal using canonical format with both date and time components. You can optionally use `T` or `t` as a separator between the date and time parts.

```googlesql
DATETIME '2014-09-27 12:30:00.45'
```
