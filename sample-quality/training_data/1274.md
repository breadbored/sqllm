How do I add statistical noise to query results for privacy protection?

--- AGENT ---

The `DIFFERENTIAL_PRIVACY` clause adds calibrated noise to aggregation results, protecting individual privacy while enabling aggregate analysis. You specify epsilon (noise level), delta (failure probability), and privacy unit columns.

```googlesql
SELECT
  WITH DIFFERENTIAL_PRIVACY
    OPTIONS(epsilon=10, delta=.01, max_groups_contributed=2)
    item,
    AVG(quantity, contribution_bounds_per_group => (0,100)) AS average_quantity
FROM professors
GROUP BY item;
```
