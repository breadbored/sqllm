How do I truncate a date to the beginning of its ISO year?

--- AGENT ---

`DATE_TRUNC()` with ISOYEAR granularity rounds a date down to the first day of its ISO 8601 week-numbering year, which differs from the Gregorian calendar year boundary. Combined with `EXTRACT(ISOYEAR)`, you can verify which ISO year a date belongs to.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATE_TRUNC('2015-06-15', ISOYEAR) AS isoyear_boundary,
  EXTRACT(ISOYEAR FROM DATE '2015-06-15') AS isoyear_number;
```
