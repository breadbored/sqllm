How do I construct a TIMESTAMP from a string that includes both an embedded time zone and a different explicit time zone?

--- AGENT ---

When a timestamp string includes a UTC offset or time zone literal (like "UTC"), do not provide a separate time zone parameter to the `TIMESTAMP` function, as the embedded time zone takes precedence for interpreting the civil time.

```googlesql
SELECT TIMESTAMP("2008-12-25 15:30:00 UTC") AS timestamp_str;
```
