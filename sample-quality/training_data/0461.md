--- USER ---

How do I compare the difference in Gregorian years versus ISO years between two DATETIME values?

--- AGENT ---

DATETIME_DIFF with YEAR counts Gregorian calendar year boundaries, while ISOYEAR counts ISO 8601 week-numbering year boundaries. This example shows how the same date range yields different results depending on which year standard is used.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATETIME_DIFF('2017-12-30 00:00:00',
    '2014-12-30 00:00:00', YEAR) AS year_diff,
  DATETIME_DIFF('2017-12-30 00:00:00',
    '2014-12-30 00:00:00', ISOYEAR) AS isoyear_diff;
```
