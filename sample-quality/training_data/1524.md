How do I extract both ISO week and regular week numbers from the same timestamp?

--- AGENT ---

You can extract both `ISOYEAR`, `ISOWEEK` (ISO 8601 standard), and regular `YEAR`, `WEEK` components from a single timestamp in one query. The ISO week system begins on Monday and may have different boundaries than the standard Gregorian week system.

```googlesql
SELECT
  EXTRACT(ISOYEAR FROM TIMESTAMP("2005-01-03 12:34:56+00")) AS isoyear,
  EXTRACT(ISOWEEK FROM TIMESTAMP("2005-01-03 12:34:56+00")) AS isoweek,
  EXTRACT(YEAR FROM TIMESTAMP("2005-01-03 12:34:56+00")) AS year,
  EXTRACT(WEEK FROM TIMESTAMP("2005-01-03 12:34:56+00")) AS week
```
