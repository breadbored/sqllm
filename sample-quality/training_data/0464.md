How do I truncate a DATETIME value to the beginning of a day?

--- AGENT ---

DATETIME_TRUNC rounds a DATETIME to the beginning of the specified granularity. This example truncates the datetime to the start of the day, removing the time component.

```googlesql
SELECT
  DATETIME "2008-12-25 15:30:00" as original,
  DATETIME_TRUNC(DATETIME "2008-12-25 15:30:00", DAY) as truncated;
```
