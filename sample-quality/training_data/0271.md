--- USER ---

How does PARSE_BIGNUMERIC handle rounding when there are too many decimal digits?

--- AGENT ---

PARSE_BIGNUMERIC rounds half away from zero when the number of digits after the decimal point exceeds the maximum of 38 supported by BIGNUMERIC.

```googlesql
SELECT PARSE_BIGNUMERIC("1.123456789012345678901234567890123456789") as parsed;
```
